Purpose of Uni-verse

The purpose of the Uni-verse is to educate the college student population about the benefits of the metaverse and what it means for us as college students. Currently, more and more comapanies are investing into the metaverse hoping to make it happen, and for where we are right now, the metaverse doesn't seem too far off. Thus, it's important for all of us to be aware of this hot technological trend.
